CIVIL PROJECTS

Sammut Marine Group is a leading marine and land-based civil engineering firm, renowned in Malta and internationally. ​ Sammut Marine Group is honoured to be working on several projects both in Malta and abroad which range from warehousing, a showroom on four levels including car park and storage, to blocks of residential accommodations including garages, apartments, maisonettes, penthouses, terraced houses and villas for the development of air-spaces which include penthouses and apartments. As a firm, we are also involved in several marine projects and sea-based structures whilst working on small scale tasks such as regularazation of applications, renovation of apartments and penthouses, together with projects located in ODZ development such as the construction of reservoirs, agricultural storage and pump rooms. As a structural and civil engineering firm having a section dedicated to interior design, we follow projects from conception to completion, starting with conceptual design, full architectural design, submission at the Planning Authority (PA), and correspondance with all local entities such as ERA, SCH, MTA, etc. Civil engineering design including foundations and structural design, project supervision on site, project management and interior design.
